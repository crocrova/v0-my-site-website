Our Story

Three generations
of Italian craft

Salvatore G's was born from the Galluzzo family's love for Italian cooking — the kind passed down at the family table, made with care, shared without reservation. They brought it to Cabo San Lucas and found the perfect home: a sun-drenched courtyard, loyal guests, and a city that knows good food when it tastes it.

Every recipe carries that history. Homemade pastas, slow-cooked sauces, Italian porketta — each dish is a statement that food should be generous, communal, and unforgettable.
